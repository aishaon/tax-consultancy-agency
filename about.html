Who We Are

Trusted Tax & Financial Partner Since 2010

Ajay & Safiullah Associates is a premier NBR-approved VAT and Tax Consultancy Firm based in Rangpur, Bangladesh. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional tax, accounting, and business registration services to individuals, startups, and established enterprises across northern Bangladesh.

Our team of highly skilled and experienced consultants brings deep expertise in Bangladesh tax laws, VAT regulations, corporate compliance, and financial management. We pride ourselves on delivering personalized, confidential, and results-oriented solutions that help our clients achieve financial compliance and business success.

Located at Samabay Market in Rangpur, we serve clients from all over Rangpur Division and beyond. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted consultancy firms in the region.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every aspect of our work and client relationships.

Our Mission

To simplify tax compliance and financial management for individuals and businesses in Bangladesh through professional, reliable, and affordable consultancy services. We strive to make tax and financial processes accessible and stress-free for all our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading tax and financial consultancy firm in northern Bangladesh, recognized for our expertise, integrity, and commitment to client success. We aim to set the standard for professional consultancy services in the region.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, excellence, confidentiality, and client-centricity. These core values define who we are and how we serve our clients. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust and mutual respect.
